---
layout: recipe
title: "Vodka Sour"
date: 2022-07-16
recipe:
  source_name: Mariah McIntosh
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 2 oz. vodka
    * 1 tsp. maple syrup
    * one egg white
    * juice from one or two tiny oranges, lemons, or limes
    * cardamom bitters

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. shake together egg white, citrus juice, vodka, cardamom bitters, and syrup
    2. strain and pour over ice

  notes_markdown: |-
    Makes two drinks.
    Good with strawberry basil.
---
