title: "Scallion Tofu"
date: 2020-03-08
recipe:
  servings: 4 servings
  prep: 15 min.
  cook: 15 min.
  source_name: Helen Le
  source_publisher: Helen's Recipes
  source_url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UgreUS9-Sig
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    ## tofu:

    * 1 block firm tofu
    * vegetable oil for frying

    ## scallion sauce:

    * 1 tbsp. sugar
    * 2 tbsp. light soy sauce
    * 2 tbsp. water
    * 1/4 tsp. mushroom seasoning powder
    * 2 tbsp. chopped scallion

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. wrap tofu in paper towels and press under heavy object for a few minutes
    2. cut into 1.5 inch cubes
    3. heat oil until bubbles form around a chopstick
    4. fry tofu until golden on all sides
    5. drain on paper towels
    6. combine sauce ingredients in a bowl
    7. dip hot tofu into scallion sauce and remove immediately
       * tofu absorbs sauce better while still hot
       * scallion will cling to the pieces
    8. serve with rice
