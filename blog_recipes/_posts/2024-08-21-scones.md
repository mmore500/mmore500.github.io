title: "Scones"
date: 2024-08-21
recipe:
  servings: 8 scones
  prep: 40 min.
  cook: 20 min.
  preheat: 400 F
  source_publisher: Love and Lemons
  source_url: https://www.loveandlemons.com/scones-recipe/
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 2 c. flour
    * 1/4 c. sugar
    * 2 tsp. baking powder
    * 1/2 tsp. salt
    * 1 stick butter, frozen
    * 1/2 c. buttermilk, cold
    * 1 egg
    * 1 tsp. vanilla (optional)
    * coarse sugar for topping

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
    2. grate frozen butter on box grater
    3. toss butter into flour and work with hands until coarse crumbs
    4. add mix-ins if using
    5. whisk buttermilk, egg, and vanilla separately
    6. pour wet into dry and stir until shaggy
    7. knead briefly until dough comes together
    8. pat into 1-inch thick disk
    9. cut into 8 wedges
    10. freeze 15 minutes
    11. brush with buttermilk and sprinkle coarse sugar
    12. bake 20 minutes until golden

  notes_markdown: |-
    for flakier scones, fold dough in thirds twice before shaping

---
