title: "Tofu Tacos"
date: 2020-03-08
recipe:
  prep: 30 min.
  cook: 20 min.
  source_name: Helen Le
  source_publisher: Helen's Recipes
  source_url: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UgreUS9-Sig
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * fried tofu
    * avocado
    * scallions
    * green apple slices
    * shredded dried seaweed
    * sauteed mushrooms OR bread and butter pickles
    * corn tortillas
  directions_markdown: |-
    1. press tofu and cut into strips or cubes
    2. pan fry tofu in oil until golden and crispy on all sides
    3. if using mushrooms, saute until golden
    4. warm corn tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over flame
    5. slice avocado and green apple into thin slices
    6. assemble tacos: layer tofu, avocado, apple slices, scallions, and seaweed
    7. add sauteed mushrooms or bread and butter pickles
    8. serve immediately
