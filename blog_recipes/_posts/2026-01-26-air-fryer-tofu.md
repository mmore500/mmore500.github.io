title: "Air Fryer Tofu"
date: 2026-01-26
recipe:
  servings: 4 servings
  prep: 20 min.
  cook: 15 min.
  source_publisher: Jessica in the Kitchen
  source_url: https://jessicainthekitchen.com/how-to-make-crispy-air-fryer-tofu/
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 1 lb. extra firm tofu (16 oz.), pressed
    * 2 tbsp. soy sauce or tamari
    * 1 tbsp. sesame oil
    * 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
    * 2 tbsp. cornstarch
    * 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
    * 1/2 tsp. onion powder
    * 1/2 tsp. paprika
    * 1/4 tsp. sea salt
    * 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. press tofu for 15 to 30 minutes to remove excess moisture
       * use a tofu press or wrap in clean towel with something heavy on top
    2. cut pressed tofu into 1-inch cubes or tear into pieces
    3. transfer tofu cubes to a large bowl
    4. drizzle soy sauce, sesame oil, and rice vinegar over tofu
    5. toss to coat evenly
    6. sprinkle cornstarch and all seasonings over tofu
    7. toss thoroughly to combine
    8. place tofu in a single layer in air fryer basket
       * do not overcrowd, work in batches if needed
    9. air fry at 400 F for 10 to 15 minutes
    10. shake basket after 10 minutes
    11. tofu is ready when golden and crispy
    12. serve immediately

  notes_markdown: |-
    Pressing the tofu is essential for crispy results. For extra flavor, marinate the tofu longer before adding cornstarch. Can substitute arrowroot starch for cornstarch.
