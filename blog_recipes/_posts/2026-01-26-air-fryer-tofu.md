---

title: "Air Fryer Tofu"
date: 2026-01-26
recipe:
  servings: 4 servings
  prep: 20 min.
  cook: 15 min.
  source_publisher: Jessica in the Kitchen
  source_url: https://jessicainthekitchen.com/how-to-make-crispy-air-fryer-tofu/
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    * 16 oz. extra firm tofu
    * 2 tbsp. soy sauce
    * 1 tbsp. sesame oil
    * 1 tbsp. rice vinegar
    * 2 tbsp. cornstarch
    * 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
    * 1/2 tsp. onion powder
    * 1/4 tsp. paprika
    * salt and pepper

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. press tofu 15 minutes with towel and heavy object
    2. cut into 1-inch cubes
    3. toss tofu with soy sauce, sesame oil, and vinegar
    4. add cornstarch and seasonings, toss to coat
    5. arrange in single layer in air fryer basket
    6. cook at 400 F for 10-15 minutes, shaking halfway
    7. serve when golden and crispy

  notes_markdown: |-
    work in batches if needed to avoid crowding
