title: "Milk Bar Pie"
date: 2024-05-30
recipe:
  servings: 2 pies
  prep: 1 hr.
  cook: 25 min.
  preheat: 350 F
  source_name: Christina Tosi
  source_publisher: Momofuku Milk Bar
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    ## oat cookie:

    * 1 stick butter, softened
    * 1/3 c. brown sugar
    * 3 tbsp. white sugar
    * 1 egg yolk
    * 1/2 c. flour
    * 1 1/2 c. rolled oats
    * 1/8 tsp. baking powder
    * pinch baking soda
    * 1/2 tsp. salt

    ## crust:

    * oat cookie crumbs
    * 1 tbsp. brown sugar
    * 1/4 tsp. salt
    * 4 tbsp. melted butter

    ## filling:

    * 1 1/2 c. white sugar
    * 3/4 c. brown sugar
    * 1/4 c. milk powder
    * 1 1/2 tsp. salt
    * 1 c. melted butter
    * 3/4 c. heavy cream
    * 1/2 tsp. vanilla
    * 8 egg yolks

  directions_markdown: |-
    ## oat cookie:

    1. cream butter and sugars until fluffy
    2. beat in egg yolk until pale
    3. mix in flour, oats, baking powder, baking soda, and salt
    4. spread thin on parchment-lined sheet
    5. bake 15 minutes until golden
    6. cool completely

    ## crust:

    1. pulse cookie with brown sugar and salt in food processor
    2. add melted butter and knead into ball
    3. press into 2 pie tins, bottom and sides

    ## filling:

    1. combine sugars, milk powder, and salt in stand mixer on low
    2. add melted butter and mix 2 minutes
    3. add cream and vanilla, mix until combined
    4. add yolks and paddle until smooth
       * keep speed low to avoid adding air

    ## baking:

    1. fill crusts three-quarters full
    2. bake 15 minutes at 350 F
    3. reduce to 325 F and bake 5 more minutes
    4. cool, then freeze at least 3 hours
    5. dust with powdered sugar to serve

  notes_markdown: |-
    must freeze before serving for proper texture
