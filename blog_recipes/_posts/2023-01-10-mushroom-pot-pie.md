---
layout: recipe
title: "Mushroom Pot Pie"
date: 2023-01-10
recipe:
  servings: 6 servings
  prep: 10 min.
  cook: 35 min.
  preheat: 350 F
  source_publisher: Simply Delicious Food
  source_url: https://simply-delicious-food.com/creamy-mushroom-pot-pie/
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    ## filling:

    * 750g mixed mushrooms, roughly chopped
    * 2 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
    * 5 sprigs fresh thyme, leaves removed
    * 2 tbsp. flour
    * 200ml vegetable stock
    * 200ml cream
    * squeeze of fresh lemon juice
    * olive oil or butter
    * salt and pepper to taste

    ## pie:

    * 1 roll ready-made puff pastry, thawed
    * 1 egg yolk, beaten

  directions_markdown: |-
    1. preheat oven to 350 F (180 C)
    2. saute mushrooms in olive oil or butter until golden brown
       * work in batches if needed to avoid crowding
    3. add garlic and thyme, cook 1 minute
    4. stir in flour to coat mushrooms
    5. pour in stock and cream, whisk until smooth
    6. simmer 5 to 10 minutes until thickened
    7. season with lemon juice, salt, and pepper
    8. transfer filling to individual oven-proof ramekins
    9. roll pastry on floured surface
    10. cut circles to fit ramekins
    11. press pastry onto ramekins, crimp edges
    12. cut small slits in pastry for steam to escape
    13. brush with beaten egg yolk
    14. bake 15 to 20 minutes until pastry is golden
    15. cool 5 minutes before serving
