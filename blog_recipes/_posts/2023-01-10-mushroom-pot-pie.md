---
layout: recipe
title: "Mushroom Pot Pie"
date: 2023-01-10
recipe:
  servings: 6 servings
  prep: 10 min.
  cook: 35 min.
  preheat: 350 F
  source_publisher: Simply Delicious Food
  source_url: https://simply-delicious-food.com/creamy-mushroom-pot-pie/
  ingredients_markdown: |-
    ## filling:

    * 1.5 lbs. mixed mushrooms, chopped
    * 2 cloves garlic, sliced
    * 1 tbsp. fresh thyme leaves
    * 2 tbsp. flour
    * 1 c. vegetable stock
    * 1 c. cream
    * lemon juice
    * butter or oil
    * salt and pepper

    ## topping:

    * 1 sheet puff pastry
    * 1 egg yolk

  directions_markdown: |-
    ## filling:

    1. brown mushrooms in butter, working in batches
    2. add garlic and thyme, cook 1 minute
    3. stir in flour
    4. pour in stock and cream, whisk smooth
    5. simmer until thick, about 10 minutes
    6. season with lemon, salt, and pepper

    ## assembly:

    1. divide filling into ramekins
    2. cut pastry circles to fit
    3. press pastry over ramekins and crimp edges
    4. cut slits for steam
    5. brush with egg yolk
    6. bake 20 minutes until golden
